Deonadi Project

Deonadi river watershed covers the majority part of Sinnar taluka which falls under scarcity zone. The project is being carried out to see the stream flow pattern over the years and aims to develop a decision support system for better water management using PoCRA.

Rural Water Supply Scheme

RWSS is implemented in different villages of Palghar and Thane. The scheme includes the construction MBR, ESR I, ESR II, transmission main, gravity main, pump house, rising main, pumping machinery, distribution network and MSEB connection for energy need. TDSC has been empaneled for assessing this scheme
